First Shubie

Paul Maxan
2023-04-07

Shubenacadie River, from Maitland to Green Creek Flooding: First paddle of the season. There was no ice in the river, still a lot of fresh water due to the melting snow. The strait still has ice, from the bridge you could see almost a 3rd covered. It wasn’t the biggest flow and there was no bore. We did not catch a lot of features and Chris’s opinion was that we were too late for most.
